A message from Ernie

Building a brighter future—together

As a professional baseball player, I’ve been fortunate to live my dream. Now I’m asking fans like you to help strengthen local youth baseball and support cancer research—over the next three to four years, with your partnership.

Support the project Learn more

Youth baseball & teamwork

I’ll be honest with you: I can’t do this without people who believe that a kid with a glove deserves a real shot, and that no family should face cancer alone. If something on this page speaks to you—if you remember someone who cheered you on, or someone you’re still rooting for—your gift helps turn that belief into equipment, coaching, hope, and research. Thank you, from the bottom of my heart, for even being here.

Why this project matters

Youth sports teach life skills. Research saves lives. Community brings us together. Here’s what your support makes possible.

  1. 01

    Empowering the next generation

    Youth baseball is more than a game—it builds teamwork, perseverance, and discipline. Your support helps young athletes get the resources they need to thrive on and off the field.

  2. 02

    Fighting cancer

    Cancer touches too many families. Funding research drives new treatments and saves lives. Every contribution helps move that work forward.

  3. 03

    Stronger communities

    Sports connect people. Stronger local programs mean more places for families to gather, share experiences, and belong.

  4. 04

    Leading by example

    Fans lifted me up along the way. Together we can show the next generation what it means to give back.

Project commitment

Total donations are planned to be returned with 70% interest at the completion of the project.

Details and timing will be confirmed as the project reaches its milestones.

Where your gift goes

Contributions go toward improving youth baseball programs—training equipment, coaching, and scholarships for underprivileged kids—alongside cancer research initiatives that are critical in the fight against this disease.

Online gifts start at $100. What matters is standing together for young athletes and for everyone affected by cancer.

  • ⚾ Equipment & safe facilities for youth programs
  • 🧢 Coaching and development opportunities
  • 🎓 Scholarships for underprivileged players
  • 🔬 Funding for cancer research initiatives

Make a donation

When you give, you’re not filling out a form—you’re standing with kids on the field and with families in the fight. That means more to us than words can say.

Thank you for reading and for considering a gift. Your generosity helps change lives and strengthens our community.

With gratitude,
Ernie
